					<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="dablink">This article is about champions' initial statistics.&#32;&#32;For general statistics, see <a href="/wiki/Champion_statistic" title="Champion statistic">champion statistic</a>.</div>
<p>The <b>base champion statistics</b> are the initial <a href="/wiki/Champion_statistic" title="Champion statistic">statistics</a> that the <a href="/wiki/Champion" title="Champion">champions</a> begin with.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Statistics"> Statistics </span><span class="editsection"><a href="/wiki/Base_champion_statistics?action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit Statistics section"><img src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" class="sprite edit-pencil" />Edit</a></span></h2>
<p>The values are the stats the champion would start with at level 0, and the immediate column to the right represents how much it increases for each level (denoted by a +). Abilities that alter base stats (such as <span class="character_icon" style="white-space: nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Tristana" title="Tristana"><img alt="" src="http://static1.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20091218195502/leagueoflegends/images/thumb/0/06/TristanaSquare.png/20px-TristanaSquare.png" width="20" height="20" data-image-name="TristanaSquare.png" data-image-key="TristanaSquare.png" /></a>&#160;<span><a href="/wiki/Tristana" title="Tristana">Tristana's</a></span></span> <span style="white-space: nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Tristana#Draw_a_Bead" title="Draw a Bead"><img alt="" src="http://static2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20091209220104/leagueoflegends/images/thumb/b/b5/Draw_a_Bead.jpg/20px-Draw_a_Bead.jpg" width="20" height="20" data-image-name="Draw a Bead.jpg" data-image-key="Draw_a_Bead.jpg" /></a>&#160;<a href="/wiki/Tristana#Draw_a_Bead" title="Tristana">Draw a Bead</a></span>) are not illustrated on this page. The MP column represents the secondary bar below the health bar and is not to be confused with resource used for abilities (e.g. Aatrox's MP represents Blood Well and Mordekaiser's MP represents Shield despite both of them using Health as their resource).
</p>
